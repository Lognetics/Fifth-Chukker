Polo Legacy

West Africa's home of high-goal polo.

Since 2001, Fifth Chukker has grown from a vision on the banks of Kangimi Dam into the beating heart of Nigerian polo — a destination where international patrons, royalty and champions gather to play the sport of kings beneath flame trees and African skies.

Our fields have hosted some of the continent's most celebrated fixtures, including the Access Bank UNICEF Charity Shield and the African Patrons Cup — competitions that pair world-class high-goal polo with a purpose far greater than the game.
