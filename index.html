Our Story · Est. 2001

A polo legacy carved
into the African plains.

Set within a 3,000-hectare resort on the banks of Kangimi Dam, Fifth Chukker has grown from a single field into West Africa's premier polo destination — where the Sport of Kings, world-class hospitality and authentic African warmth meet.

From the Access Bank UNICEF Charity Shield to the African Patrons Cup, our fields have hosted international patrons, royalty and champions — all while championing causes that change lives across the continent.
